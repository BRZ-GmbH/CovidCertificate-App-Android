Privacy statement & Terms and conditions of use

As part of the "Grüner Pass App" no personal data is processed by Austrian Federal Computing Center (Bundesrechenzentrum GmbH) or the Federal Ministry for Social Affairs, Health, Care and Consumer Protection. All processing takes place exclusively on the user's device and exclusively due to his/her independent import and independent management of the certificates within the app. Neither the Bundesrechenzentrum GmbH nor the Federal Ministry are therefore to be regarded as those responsible for data protection according to Article 4 (7) of the EU General Data Protection Regulation. Only the data imported by the user is processed on the end device. A correction can only be made by the user him/herself. The imported data will be deleted at the latest when the app is removed from the device. The imported data is always portable, otherwise it would not be possible to import it into the app. You can object to processing by removing the relevant certificate or app from the device.
